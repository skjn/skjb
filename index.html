About Me

Brief introduction

"God Lies In The Details."

Architect turned UX designer, I have worked across multiple design fields. Being passionate about design, I have been designing physical and virtual experiences for people for last 7 years .It has helped me understand how people respond to forms, colors, shapes and other intangible aspects. I feel that the perfect balance and harmony achieved in design through user research is what converts a great design into the perfect one.
My interests lie in the field of designing interfaces and interactions for education and learning technologies, developing online collaborative work environments and productivity softwares. I am also interested in designing for upcoming technologies like VR to integrate in education + collaborative environments.
